About Career Development Centre

  • The Career Development Centre in our Institute is a Resource Centre for Information, Guidance, and Counselling. CDC aims to groom students overall and make them placement ready. It provides substantial guidance for all the opportunities ahead of them.

    • Vision

  • To maintain a dynamic equilibrium between corporates and the campus by producing quality frontline human resources for the industries and society who will cater to the prosperity of our nation through excellence in technical education and research.

    • Mission

  • To create quality frontline human resources to serve the nation logically, economically, and ethically.
  • To generate knowledge-based technological upliftments and services through strong interaction between industries and campus.
  • To provide wings to the aspirations of every individual by guiding them to reach their prime destination through the realization of their capabilities and providing ample support.

    • CDC Activities

    Career Information

    • Provide information about professional development courses and higher studies opportunities available in India and abroad.
    • To know, understand and sharpen students’ interests and plans for a satisfying future.

    Career Assessment

    • Interpretation of career and psychometric assessments tools such as DAT, Strong Interest Inventory, etc.

    Career Guidance and Counseling

    • Career Counseling
    • Group Counseling
    • Personal Counseling
    • Drop-In Advising.

    Career Skill Training

    • Interpretation of career and psychometric assessments tools such as DAT, Strong Interest Inventory, etc.

    Career Guidance and Counseling

    • Organize mock group discussions, mock interviews, resume preparation, webinars on profile buildings, conduct training programs on behavioral skills and personality developments, etc.


    Chairman's Desk

    Progression and improvement of science and technology are seen all around the globe. It calls for personnel to imbibe technological evolution and adapt to new possibilities. At Bit Sindri, the academic curriculum follows innovative learning over traditional teaching techniques to cope with this pace.


    Our students are among the best minds in the country selected through the competitive exam JEE MAIN. Our institution provides them the platform to inoculate their skills in this technology–rich arena. We teach our students so that they become problem solvers of real-world issues and can apply their domain knowledge outside the bookish words.


    In shaping their young minds, we create able future leaders who are skilled, creative, and out-of-the-box thinkers. We focus on students’ overall development, from improving their skills to imparting them with the best quality education.


    With these clear objectives and being one of the premier institutions of the state, I can assure you that the budding engineers of BIT Sindri are meritorious and will prove to be an asset to any organization.

    Invitation to Recruiters

    Dear Sir/Madam,

    Greetings from Placement Cell, BIT Sindri


    It gives me immense pleasure to extend to you our most cordial invitation to participate in the Campus Recruitment Programmes of our institute. BIT Sindri is one of the oldest and premier institutions in India under the Department of Higher & Technical Education, Jharkhand. It is affiliated with AICTE and accredited with the NBA. Since its inception in 1949, the college has produced numerous global leaders in engineering and is one of the front runners in the field of research.


    The institute aims to continue its legacy by creating quality technocrats who can substantially become frontline leaders. The institute has 10 engineering departments (Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Production Engineering, Metallurgical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Information Technology, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Chemical Engineering, and Mining Engineering). The institute also has five basic science departments, namely, Physics, Chemistry, Geology, Mathematics, and Humanities. We offer Undergraduate (B.Tech), Post Graduate (M.Tech), and Doctoral programs.


    Apart from these programs, the institute offers a wide range of research and development programs and has a Centre for Excellencein collaboration with Siemens. Our students are one of the best pools of students selected through a rigorous selection procedure of JEE (Joint Entrance Examinations). We proudly house a pool of the country’s finest brains. The college is known for its widespread alumni network. We are equipped with highly qualified faculties, an updated curriculum, a technology-driven environment for collaborative learning, good infrastructure, and a lush green campus.


    Our previous placement sessions witnessed an exponential rise in the placement offers and internships for our students. The placement record is a testimony to the corporates’ growing belief in our students. We would like to have your benign presence on our campus for the recruitment drive of the students of the 2023 passing out batch and internship opportunities for the 2024 passing out batch. I assure you that our students will not just meet your expectations but substantially cross them. Looking forward to a mutually beneficial and long-term relationship.
