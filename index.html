About the Institute

Established in 1949, BIT Sindri is one of India's oldest government engineering and technological institutes. BIT Sindri is known for its contribution to the field of higher technical education and research.

Read More





Vision

To provide the valuable human resources for the industry and society through the excellence in technical education and scientific research for the sustainable development.

Mission

To offer state-of-the-art Undergraduate, Postgraduate and Doctoral programs, generate new knowledge by quality research, undertake the collaborative projects with industries and society, develop human intellectual capacity with its fullest potential and solve the problems of society through innovation in technology.

Message From Director

Prof.Pankaj Rai

Director, BIT Sindri

Welcome to BIT Sindri, a pioneer in technical education with over seven decades of excellence. We take pride in offering our students a transformative education that equips them to be well-rounded, socially responsible, and forward-thinking leaders. At BIT Sindri, we believe education is more than just acquiring knowledge; it’s about developing the skills and mindset necessary to succeed in a rapidly changing world. That’s why we emphasize multidisciplinary learning, creativity, and critical thinking. Our dedicated faculty members provide quality instruction in a supportive environment, encouraging students to reach their full potential.
