Admission Procedure

Admission to B.Tech Programs:

  • Admissions to all B.Tech programs are made through JEE Main Examination followed by Jharkhand Combined Entrance Competitive Examination (JCECE) councelling conducted by JCECE Board constituted by Govt of Jharkhand. Lateral entry to diploma holders in engineering and technology is made in the 3rd semester through competitive examination conducted separately for them. The number of seats through lateral entry in all branches are 10% of the normal seats.
  • For more details, please see JCECE

    • Admission to M.Tech Programs:

  • Admissions to M.Tech programs are made through written test/interview conducted by the institute. Candidates with a valid GATE score with B.E. /B.Tech or equivalent in respective discipline with a minimum of 60% marks or equivalent C.G.P.A ( in case of SC /ST the minimum marks of 55% or equivalent C.G.P.A) from recognized University/Institute, are eligible to apply in prescribed format
  • Candidates possessing above qualifications and having valid GATE score will be entitled for monthly stipend as per the A.I.C.T.E. / Government approved norms. If seats are not filled up with GATE qualified candidates for M.Tech program, other candidates may also be considered without monthly stipend.

    • Admission to Research Programs:

  • Admission to Ph.D (Engineering) program in various engineering disciplines and interdisciplinary area is made through written test/interview conducted by the institute. Candidates with M.E. /M.Tech or equivalent degree in respective discipline with a minimum of 60% marks or equivalent C.G.P.A ( in case of SC /ST the minimum marks of 55% or equivalent C.G.P.A) from recognized University/Institute, are eligible to apply in prescribed format.
  • Candidates possessing above qualifications and having valid GATE score will be entitled for monthly stipend as per the A.I.C.T.E. / Government approved norms.
