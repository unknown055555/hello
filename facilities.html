Facilities

Deshpande Auditorium:

  • BIT Sindri comes up with the most extraordinary spectacle in the DPA building. The Deshpande Auditorium is named after the founding Director of BIT Sindri. It has two levels of seating on the ground floor and the gallery respectively. Various events, shows, and competitions are held on its premises.

    • Sports Grounds:

  • BIT Sindri has two enormous grounds for students to play sports like Cricket, volleyball, football, basketball, etc. There are separate grounds for boys and girls. The ground for girls consists of a basketball court and it is in the vicinity of the girls’ hostel. Inter-branch sports competitions are often conducted on these grounds.

    • Gymkhana:

  • The campus of BIT Sindri comes up with a Gym Facility. The gym of Bit Sindri has a sufficient number of fitness machines and weights. It has adequate facilities for weight training and bodybuilding which is more than enough for a student to maintain a healthy life.

    • Health care center :

  • Medical facilities are also provided in BIT Sindri. There is a Dispensary on the college campus, just behind the 1st-year lecture Hall. Where Doctors are available and checkup is provided for free.

    • Sports facilities in GH :

  • BIT Sindri takes care of the recreation and refreshment of all students, so different sports facilities are provided inside the girls’ hostel as well.
  • Counting on indoor games, a table tennis set is provided in the common room of the hostel, and for outdoor games, a badminton court and volleyball court are provided in the lobby of many hostels.

    • Lecture Hall:

  • All students of B.tech first year assemble in the Lecture Hall complex to attend their respective lectures. The Lecture Hall Complex has three floors of Lecture Halls which are well furnished and have smart teaching technology installed in them.

    • Library:

  • The Central Library of BIT Sindri is well equipped to impart knowledge of their liking. It houses course books, novels, Competitive examination guides, and many more quality books. There is a noiseless environment in the Library premises in which students can study. Also, the facility to borrow books for a stipulated amount of time is also provided.

    • Canteen:

  • In order to ensure BITians don’t crave delicious foods while being far apart from their homes and have a spot to spend their leisure time BIT has a canteen that serves delicacies of various cultural diversity right from Chinese to south Indian.
